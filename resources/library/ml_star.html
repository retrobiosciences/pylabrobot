‘Hamilton_96_tiprack_50ul_NTR’
Formats:
- “50μL CO-REII Tips, stacked NTRs, sterile”: Part no.: 235987

- “50uL CO-REII Nested Clear Tips”: Part no.: 235964

    • Sterile=Non-Sterile
